What is Addiction?

Addiction is a chronic brain disorder… with the right help, recovery is possible.

Fatigue, organ damage, withdrawal symptoms

Social Consequences

Broken bonds, isolation, trust issues

Debt, job instability, financial struggle

Acknowledgement

The first step is recognizing the problem. Denial fades, and acceptance begins the path to healing.

Being part of a community that understands, supports, and uplifts creates a foundation for long-term recovery.

Setting new goals, mending relationships, finding purpose.
